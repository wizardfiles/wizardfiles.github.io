About Armada Aeronautics

Today, two of the largest consumer problems in the world are:
- Increasing traffic times
- Housing prices
A person, on average, spends a whole work week per year in traffic.
A person pays as much as 10 times higher for an old house in a popular location than for a mansion with a garden only 30 miles away.
Armada designed a transportation infrastructure that suppose to solve both problems for consumers! And we made sure consumer would pay even less than what they pay today!

We, in Armada, designed a modern, 21st century transportation aircraft from scratch.
Designed for modern needs and modern ridesharing economy.
Designed with autonomous mode in mind.
Designed green and better for the environment than any EV car.

Advances in software and microhardware changed our lives in the 21st century, but they haven’t made their way into aviation.
Aviation industry is about 50 years behind.

We, the founders of Armada, stay for hours in traffic, same as you.
We the founders of Armada, can barely afford rent in our city, same as you.
We, the founders of Armada, and our kids, are breathing gasoline exhaust from the cars and airplanes, same as you.
We are fixing it. Now! We created a ride that is clean, enjoyable, fast, and safe. Come along with us.
